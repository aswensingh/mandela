# Bright Smile Dental Studio — Policies

## Cancellation
- Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the appointment.
- Less than 24 hours' notice: 50% of consultation fee charged at next visit.
- No-show without notice: RM 50 flat fee.

## Warranty
- Composite fillings: 1 year against fracture or fall-out (excluding new decay).
- Porcelain crowns and veneers: 3 years against breakage under normal use.
- Implants: 5-year warranty on the implant fixture; lifetime warranty on the
  surgical placement (re-do at no charge if osseointegration fails within 6 months).

## Pregnancy
- We will not take dental X-rays during the first trimester unless urgent.
- Routine cleaning and check-ups are safe and recommended during the second
  trimester. Please tell us at booking time.

## Privacy
- Patient records are stored under PDPA 2010. We never share data with third
  parties without consent, except where required by law.
