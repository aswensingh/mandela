# Bright Smile Dental Studio — Frequently Asked Questions

## Opening hours
- Monday to Friday: 9:30 AM – 7:30 PM
- Saturday: 9:30 AM – 5:00 PM
- Sunday & Public Holidays: Closed
- Last appointment is accepted 1 hour before closing.

## Appointments
- We accept walk-ins but appointments are strongly recommended.
- To book, message us on WhatsApp with your full name, preferred date, and the
  service you need. A teammate will confirm within 2 working hours.
- Rescheduling is free if done at least 24 hours in advance.
- No-show fee: RM 50, charged at your next visit.

## Payment & insurance
- Accepted: cash, all major credit/debit cards, DuitNow QR, GrabPay, Touch 'n Go.
- Instalment plans available for treatments above RM 2,000 (0% interest, 6 months,
  Maybank / CIMB / Public Bank credit cards only).
- Direct billing: AIA, Great Eastern, Allianz, Prudential. Please bring your card
  and IC on the day of treatment.

## Insurance / claims
- We provide itemised receipts and medical reports for reimbursement claims.
- A signed claim form is required at least 1 working day before the appointment.

## Children
- We see patients from age 3 and up.
- Parents are welcome in the treatment room.
- We offer "happy visits" — a free 15-minute familiarisation visit for first-time
  child patients.

## Emergencies
- For severe pain, swelling, or trauma outside opening hours, call our after-hours
  line at +60 12-999 8888. A duty dentist will respond within 30 minutes.
- If you have facial swelling spreading to the eye or neck, difficulty breathing,
  or uncontrolled bleeding, go directly to the nearest A&E — do not wait.
